This _“writing through” of Vilém Flusser’s ‘The Future of Writing,’ reconfiguring it so as to become John Cayley’s ‘The Future of Language,’ _will not consider _problems concerning _any possible _future _for the _teaching _or philosophizing of an _art of _language _in the face of the growing importance of non_- or anti-linguistic _messages in our surroundings, although those problems _have already _become significant in the so-called developed countries_.
